Personal Page of Oswaldo Sevilla

I'm currently a permanent professor at the Department of Applied Matematics of UNAH . I'm currently the coordinator of the Scientific Computing Innovation Center, coordinating a Colloqium of the School of Mathematics, and part of the editorial commiittee of the journal of the Faculty of Sciences of UNAH.

I have a PhD in Algebraic Geometry from CIMAT. My dissertation topic was on Calabi-Yau threefolds fibered by elliptic K3 surfaces arising from toric varieties.

contact: osevilla@unah.edu.hn
